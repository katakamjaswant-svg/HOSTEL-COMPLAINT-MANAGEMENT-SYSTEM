How It Works

📝
1

Submit Complaint

Fill the complaint form with your details and describe the issue. You can also upload supporting images.

🎫
2

Get Complaint ID

A unique complaint ID is generated instantly. Save it to track your complaint status anytime.

🔄
3

Warden Reviews

The hostel warden reviews your complaint and assigns maintenance staff to resolve the issue quickly.

✅
4

Issue Resolved

Once resolved, the status updates to "Resolved". Track progress at every stage using your complaint ID.
